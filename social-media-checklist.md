# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist
Do’s

•	Use professional language while posting or commenting online. 

•	Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading fake news or misinformation. 

•	Maintain privacy settings on your social media accounts.

•	Use strong and unique passwords for every account. 

•	Respect others online and communicate politely. 


Don’ts

•	Do not share personal information such as phone number, address, passwords, or OTP. 

•	Do not post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.

•	Do not click suspicious links from unknown users or pages. 

•	Do not spread rumors or unverified news. 

•	Do not misuse someone else’s photos or personal data.
